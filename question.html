Why a Digital Portfolio?

Digital Portfolio's provide many benefits to the creator and the readers.


1.
Reflecion

People can better understand their own strengths and weaknesses through their Digital Portfolio's

Reflection of a person's accomplishments and hard work put into getting those accomplishments could improve their confidence in their own self-ability.
