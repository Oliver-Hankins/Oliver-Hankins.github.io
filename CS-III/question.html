Why a Digital Portfolio?

Digital Portfolio's provide many benefits to the creator and the readers.


1.
Reflecion

Digital Portfolio's allow for a person to re-evaluate all that they have done and learned, decyphering the main lessons that they obtained through the process. Also allowing for a person to track their personal growth as a person over time.

People can better understand their own strengths and weaknesses through their Digital Portfolio's

Reflection of a person's accomplishments and hard work put into getting those accomplishments could improve their confidence in their own self-ability.

2.
Story

Digital Portfolio's allow for a person to document their entire professional career, meaning that they could refer to their digital portfolio as a resume when applying for a job.

A digital portfolio allows outside viewers to better understand ones successes and failures.

3.
Skills

Finally, A digital portfolio allows for a person to develop their skills as a coder.

Digital Portfolios provide a person good practice for developing their writing style, while also letting them develop as a coder because of the large amount of html and css required in order to format and ultimately create the digital portfolio.
